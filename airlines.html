Airlines On-Time Performance Dashboard

Analysing flight delays and on-time performance by airline, route and time of day to support operations decisions.

Power BI Data Modelling KPIs
← Back to Portfolio Home

Project Overview

This project transforms raw flight data into a dashboard that helps understand delays, cancellations and on-time performance across airlines, routes and airports.

Workflow

  • Imported flight-level CSVs including departure/arrival times, delay minutes, carriers and airports.
  • Created derived fields such as delay categories, time-of-day buckets and route (origin–destination).
  • Modelled data with fact table (Flights) and dimensions (Calendar, Airline, Airport).
  • Built DAX measures for On-Time %, Average Delay, Cancellation Rate and Route Performance.
  • Designed overview, airline and route pages with slicers for date, airline and airport.

Dashboard Preview

Overview – total flights, on-time rate and delay distribution.
Airline View – performance comparison across carriers.
Route View – best and worst performing routes by delay.

Key Insights

  • Identified peak delay hours and problematic routes.
  • Compared on-time performance across airlines to highlight reliability differences.
  • Provided operations teams with clear visibility into cancellation and delay drivers.
