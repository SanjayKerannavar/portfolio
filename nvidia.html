NVIDIA Business & Product Performance Analysis

Analysing revenue, product segments and regional performance using Power BI to understand growth and profitability.

Power BI DAX Financial KPIs
← Back to Portfolio Home

Project Overview

This project focuses on analysing NVIDIA's revenue and product performance across Gaming, Data Center, Automotive and OEM segments. The goal was to create an executive-ready dashboard that tracks growth, margins and regional trends.

Workflow

  • Imported raw revenue and shipment CSVs by product, region and quarter.
  • Cleaned and standardised product lines, time periods and regional labels using Power Query.
  • Built a star-schema model with Sales (fact) and Products, Regions, Calendar (dimensions).
  • Created DAX measures: Total Revenue, YoY Growth, Gross Margin %, Segment Contribution %, ASP, and Market Growth Index.
  • Designed overview, segment and region pages with slicers for year, region and product segment.

Dashboard Preview

Company Overview – revenue, YoY growth and segment split.
Segment Performance – Gaming vs Data Center vs Automotive.
Regional Trends – revenue and growth by geography.

Key Insights

  • Identified top revenue drivers across Gaming and Data Center segments.
  • Highlighted high-growth regions benefiting from AI and cloud adoption.
  • Analysed margin differences between product lines and their impact on profitability.
